Blood seeps from torn flesh, mingling with the metallic scent of its mechanical appendages. The Slasher's body trembles, a testament to the price paid for its blind loyalty. Yet, it wears the agony as a badge of honor, convinced that redemption lies within its distorted reality.

The voice trails off, leaving behind a disconcerting silence. The Bio-Mech Slasher, caught between delusion and torment, prepares to strike with a ferocity born from twisted faith.

"Join us," the Slasher whispers, a chilling invitation laced with desperation. "Embrace the pain, for it is the gateway to transcendence. The plan will set us free."
